    • Hidden Talents

    I have always been the quiet type, and I've never really liked being in a huge spotlight. So, I surprised everyone, including myself, last year when I wrote my first song, recorded it, and released it to SoundCloud. It wasn't surprising to me that I had the technical abilities to figure out how to use Garageband to produce and record. It wasn't even surprising to me that my song had a catchy tune; I've been singing my whole life, really. It was surprising because I finally let my pent-up creativity flow freely and put it out there for the world to see, after a lifetime of putting it on the back burner. My first song accumulated a few thousand listens after a week or two, and that was excting to see that I had the ability to reach people in my own way.

    I also really enjoy editing and superimposing pictures, and making graphics. It fascinates me that technology gives me so much freedom to create. Whenever I finish a new song, its so fun for me to create the cover artwork, lyric videos, and all the promo content to post on my social media accounts.
